My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Fight Club

Unbelievable Scenes + Brad Pitt, so you know it will be amazing!

Se7en

A mind-boggling movie with an unexpected twist of events that keeps you on your toes. One of my favourite parts of the movie was when Brad Pitt said:
"What's in the box..? Tell me what's in the box!"

The Great Wall

2 American wanderers become allies of the Chinese to face the foretold legend of an attack by exotic space monsters.
